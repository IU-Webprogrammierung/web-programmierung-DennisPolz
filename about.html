About

I am a multidisciplinary designer. I love cooking and I enjoy sports. Meeting interesting individuals or visiting beautiful places on this wonderful planet is what motivates me to create. I am fascinated and inspired by a wide range of topics, including fashion, the human mind, music, health, architecture and new technologies.

My previous roles required a wide range of different competencies, which I used to develop and expand my multidisciplinary skill set. I am skilled in user experience, prototyping, interface design, design systems, brand identity, research, digital and print design, and presentations. In addition, I have a decent knowledge of sound design, photography, CMS, cutting and social media. I excel at connecting the dots and finding the missing pieces. I am passionate about learning new things.

I have had the privilege of working with a number of high-profile clients, including Porsche, Senec, the Goethe Institute, Xella, Kuka, Schaeffler Automotive, Traton SE, Kaut, Hisense, Panasonic and Rheinische Post Mediengruppe. I have written articles and shot a cover picture for a major daily newspaper, produced songs for small local and global artists, created social media strategies and graphics, designed an app for business travellers, constructed a tunnel monitoring system, published magazines and created visual media for events in Singapore and Tenerife.

I am driven by a desire to create tangible, meaningful experiences and sustainable products that last.
